About ParisRide

A Decade of Trusted
Paris Transfers

ParisRide was founded in 2014 by a team of professional Parisian drivers who were frustrated by the unreliability and hidden costs of traditional airport VTCs and shuttle buses. Our mission was simple: create the finest airport shuttle alternative Paris had ever seen.

Over the past decade, we have completed more than 15,000 transfers to Paris Charles de Gaulle, Orly, and Beauvais airports, as well as every major train station including Gare du Nord, Gare de Lyon, Gare Montparnasse, Gare de l'Est, and Gare Saint-Lazare.

Our team of 45 licensed, professional drivers operates 24 hours a day, 365 days a year. We pride ourselves on punctuality, transparent fixed pricing, and a level of professionalism that has earned us a 4.9-star average across more than 8,000 verified reviews.

  • All drivers are licensed, insured, and background-checked with the Paris Prefecture
  • Fixed flat rates agreed before every journey — no meters, no hidden fees
  • Flight tracking on all airport transfers — your driver adapts to delays automatically
  • Modern, clean vehicles maintained to the highest standards
  • Multilingual drivers — English, French, Spanish, Arabic, and more
  • Fully compliant with French VTC (Voiture de Tourisme avec Chauffeur) regulations
  • 4.9★ average rating from 8,400+ verified customer reviews
